## 1. Title & Genre

- **Title:** Pixel Vanguard (Working Title)
    
- **Genre:** Horde Survivor / Action Roguelite / Reverse Bullet Hell.
    
- **Perspective:** Top-Down 2D.
    
- **Target Platforms:**
    
    - **Android:** Google Play (Touch controls).
        
    - **Web Desktop:** Yandex Games / Itch.io (Mouse & Keyboard).
        
    - **Web Mobile:** Yandex Games (Touch controls via browser).
        

## 2. The "Elevator Pitch"

Pixel Vanguard is a kinetic, high-octane survivor game where the player fights off thousands of enemies on an infinite battlefield. Unlike similar games with stiff, retro animations, Pixel Vanguard utilizes **pre-rendered 3D-to-Pixel graphics**. This results in fluid, realistic movements and heavy combat impact, while retaining a nostalgic pixel aesthetic.

## 3. Unique Selling Point (USP): "The Meat Grinder"

The game distinguishes itself through **Game Feel** and **Visual fluidity**.

- **Fluid Pixel Realism:** The characters are not hand-drawn sprites with 4 frames of animation. They are high-quality 3D models rendered down to pixel sprites. This allows for smooth running cycles and weapon swings that look surprisingly realistic.
    
- **Kinetic Impact:** Combat is not just numbers popping up. It emphasizes **weight**. When a weapon hits, enemies are knocked back physically. The player feels like they are pushing against a tide of monsters.
    

## 4. Monetization Philosophy (Hybrid)

The game is designed to be profitable on both "Free-to-Play Web" markets and "App Stores" by using a flexible system:

- **Core Principle:** The game is fully playable for free, but paying (or watching ads) accelerates progress.
    
- **Ad-Driven Economy:** Players can "pay with time" (watching ads) to get premium features like rerolling upgrades or reviving.
    
- **IAP Economy:** Players can "pay with money" to skip the grind (buying Gold) or instantly unlock characters.
